Free-wil with grace.
God vseth not man as a brute beast or a block: but so worketh in him and by him that free-wil may concurre in euery action with his grace, which is alwaies the principal.
Heret. translation.
The heretikes to auoid this concurrence in working and labouring, translate, which is with me: where the Apostle rather saith, which laboureth with me. 32. Let vs eate and drinke.) Fasting is meritorious.
