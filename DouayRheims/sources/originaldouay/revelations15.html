1. AND I saw another signe in heauen great & maruelous: seuen Angels hauing the seuen last plagues. Because in them the wrath of God is consummate. 2. And I saw as it were ✟ Baptisme. a sea of glasse mingled with fire, & them that ouercame the beast and his image and the number of his name, standing vpon the sea of glasse, hauing the harps of God: 3. and singing ✟ The song of Moyses and Christ, is the new Testament and the old. the song of Moyses the seruant of God, and the song of the Lamb, saying: Great and maruelous are thy workes Lord God omnipotent: iust and true are thy waies King of the ✟ ' Saints worlds. 4. Who shal not feare thee, o Lord, and magnifie thy name? Because thou only art holy, because al Nations shal come, and adore in thy sight, because thy iudgements be manifest. 5. ✟ THE FIRST VISION.
