Christ answereth for the deuout woman, and for defence of her fact, as we must answer against the ignorant and il men, when they blame good men for giuing their goods to the Church. 22. Bread.
The real presence by Consecration.) This is bread before the Sacramental wordes, but the Consecration once done, of bread is made the flesh of Christ. Ambros. li. 4 c. 4 de Sacramentis. 23. Chalice.) Very bloud vnder the forme of wine.

Let vs euer giue credit to God, & neuer resist him, though the thing that he saith seeme neuer so absurd in our imagination, or farre passe al our sense and vnderstanding.
