## 3. Description (4000 char max)

```
The Douay-Rheims Bible with three classic Catholic commentaries — Haydock, Cornelius à Lapide, and the original 1609 Douai annotations. All 73 books. Completely free. No ads, no accounts, no tracking.

Tap any verse to read what the Fathers and Doctors of the Church taught about it. The commentary panel slides open right next to the text — no navigating away, no losing your place.

This is the most complete free Douay-Rheims Bible app available, built for serious reading and study.

WHAT'S INCLUDED

• Complete Douay-Rheims text — all 73 books of the Catholic canon (Challoner revision), 35,805 verses, in traditional Vulgate order
• Haydock Commentary — over 35,000 entries covering the entire Bible, drawing on Church Fathers, Doctors, and approved commentators
• Cornelius à Lapide — 2,400+ entries on the Gospels and Epistles, one of the most thorough Catholic New Testament commentaries ever written
• 1609 Douai Annotations — 3,100+ original notes from the first English Catholic Bible

FEATURES

• Full-text search across every verse
• Bookmarks — long-press any verse to save, share, or copy
• Bible in a Year reading plan covering all 73 books
• Adjustable font size and line spacing
• Dark mode with liturgical-inspired theming
• Completely offline — everything is bundled in the app

iPAD OPTIMIZED

On iPad, the app uses a three-column layout: books and navigation on the left, chapters in the center, and the reading pane with an inline commentary panel on the right. Tap a verse and the commentary opens beside the text. Read Scripture and commentary side by side, the way it was meant to be studied.

On iPhone, the app uses clean tab-based navigation with a bottom-sheet commentary panel.

PRIVACY

This app collects no data. It makes no network connections. There are no analytics, no advertising SDKs, no crash reporters, no third-party code of any kind. Your bookmarks and reading progress stay on your device and nowhere else.

WHAT THIS APP IS

A faithful digital edition of the Douay-Rheims Bible with the best traditional Catholic commentaries, built for serious reading and study. Free and open source.

WHAT THIS APP IS NOT

Not a paraphrase. Not a modern translation. Not a social Bible app. No community features, no daily devotional notifications, no gamification. Just the text, the commentaries, and you.
```
